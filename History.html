The History of BMW

From aircraft engineering to ultimate driving machines.

BMW Legacy

BMW has evolved from a small German engineering company into one of the world’s leading luxury automotive brands. Its history is built on innovation, performance, and precision engineering.

1916 – Foundation of BMW

BMW was founded in Munich, Germany, originally producing aircraft engines during World War I.

1928 – First BMW Automobile

BMW entered the automotive industry with its first car, marking the beginning of its road legacy.

1970s – Global Expansion

BMW became a global luxury brand known for sporty sedans and motorsport success.

Modern BMW Era

2000s – SUV Expansion

BMW launches the X Series, expanding into luxury SUVs.

2010s – Electric Innovation

Introduction of hybrid and electric BMW i models.

2020s – Future Mobility

BMW focuses on AI driving systems and fully electric vehicles.
